# Subscription Plans Overview

The service provides several subscription options designed to support different usage patterns and billing preferences. Customers may choose between monthly and annual billing models depending on their needs.

## Monthly subscription plans

Monthly subscriptions are billed once per billing cycle and renew automatically unless the customer cancels the subscription before the renewal date. Monthly plans provide flexibility for customers who prefer shorter commitments or who expect their service needs to change frequently.

When a monthly subscription is cancelled, access to the service continues until the end of the billing cycle that has already been paid.

## Annual subscription plans

Annual subscriptions are billed once for a full twelve-month period. Customers who choose annual billing generally receive a discounted price compared with paying the monthly rate for the same period.

Annual plans are intended for customers who expect to use the service continuously and prefer predictable long-term billing.

## Managing subscription settings

Customers can manage their subscription plan through the account settings interface. This area allows customers to view their current plan, check renewal dates, update payment information, or switch between plan types.

Plan changes may take effect immediately or at the next billing cycle depending on the nature of the change.

## Access to service features

All subscription plans provide access to the core features of the platform. Feature availability is determined by the type of subscription selected, but the underlying service environment remains the same across plans.

Additional services or optional features may be available as add-ons depending on the configuration selected by the customer.

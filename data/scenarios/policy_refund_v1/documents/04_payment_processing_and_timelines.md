# Payment Processing

Payments for subscriptions are processed through authorized payment providers when a purchase is completed.

## Payment authorization

When a customer completes a purchase, the payment provider authorizes and captures the transaction using the payment method provided during checkout. A payment confirmation message is sent once the transaction succeeds.

## Refund authorization

In cases where a refund is approved by the billing or support team, the payment reversal is submitted through the same payment provider used for the original transaction.

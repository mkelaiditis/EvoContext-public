# Cancellation and Account Closure

Customers may cancel their subscription at any time through the account management interface.

## Subscription cancellation

Cancelling a subscription prevents the service from renewing automatically at the end of the current billing cycle. The cancellation takes effect immediately, but service access continues until the end of the already-paid billing period.

Customers who cancel their subscription may reactivate their service at any time before the end of the billing period by updating their account settings.

## Account closure

Account closure is a separate process from subscription cancellation. When an account is permanently closed, access to the service platform is removed and the user's account credentials are deactivated.

Customers who wish to close their account permanently should contact customer support.

## Data retention

Certain information related to billing transactions or service usage may be retained for administrative or legal reasons even after an account has been closed. Retained records are maintained in accordance with the company's internal data retention policies.

## Re-opening an account

In some cases, previously closed accounts may be re-opened upon request if the underlying account data has not yet been permanently removed from internal systems.

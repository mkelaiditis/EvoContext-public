# Refund Policy – General Terms

This document states the standard refund policy for annual and monthly subscription purchases made through official channels.

## Cooling-off period

The 14-day cooling-off period begins on the original purchase date. Within the cooling-off window, customers are entitled to a full refund within 14 days of the original purchase, provided the service has not been substantially used during that time.

## Refund eligibility

Once the 14-day cooling-off period has passed, annual subscription fees are non-refundable. Annual subscription customers retain access to the service until the end of the paid billing period.

## Scope

This policy covers standard subscription purchases made through official service channels.

# Charge Disputes and Billing Corrections

This document covers how billing errors and incorrect charges are identified and corrected for subscription accounts. Billing correction requests are handled separately from standard subscription policy.

## Billing errors

When duplicate charges or billing errors occur, the incorrect charge will be reviewed and a refund due to error will be issued once verified. The billing team investigates duplicate charges and incorrect charge adjustments before approving corrections.

## Processing timeline

Approved corrections are processed within 5-10 business days after internal verification. The refund timeline depends on the payment method processing time and the policies of the customer's financial institution. Processing applies to verified billing error cases only.

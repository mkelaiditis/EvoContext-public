# Veridian Pro Billing Errors and Adjustments

This document explains how Veridian Software addresses billing errors for Veridian Pro. It focuses on correcting technical billing issues and duplicate charges while maintaining a consistent, fair process. It does not describe plan terms or operational payment timelines, which are documented separately.

Duplicate charges and technical billing errors are eligible for a full refund. A billing error includes scenarios where a transaction was posted more than once, or where a payment was captured despite a documented system failure. When a billing error is confirmed, Veridian Software will issue a full refund for the affected charge to the original payment method whenever possible.

To report a billing error, customers should submit the account name, the payment reference, and a brief description of the issue. Veridian Software may request screenshots or bank statements to confirm the duplicate charge or technical error. This information helps the billing team validate the claim and resolve the issue efficiently.

Service outage compensation is handled as a separate credit, not a refund. If a verified service outage results in a service impact that meets compensation criteria, the customer may receive a usage credit applied to a future invoice. This credit is distinct from billing error remediation and is managed under the service reliability guidelines.

Billing adjustments are limited to the scope of the confirmed error. They do not alter the customer’s plan level or service configuration. If an account has multiple workspaces, adjustments are applied to the specific workspace associated with the error. Veridian Software maintains a record of the adjustment and provides confirmation once the correction has been applied.

Customers are encouraged to review billing statements regularly and report issues promptly. Early reporting helps resolve issues quickly and reduces the risk of repeated errors. The billing team handles requests in a standardized manner to ensure consistent treatment across accounts.

If a customer disputes a charge that is not confirmed as a billing error, the billing team will provide a written explanation of the findings and the evidence reviewed. Customers may submit additional documentation if they believe an error exists. Veridian Software retains billing records and audit logs to support these reviews and to ensure that the final outcome is consistent with the documented facts.

This document addresses billing errors and the correction process. It does not cover other topics such as plan terms, account procedures, or processing timelines. For other questions, customers should consult the appropriate policy documents or contact support with their account information.

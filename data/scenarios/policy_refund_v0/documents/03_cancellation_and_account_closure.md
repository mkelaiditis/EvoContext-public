# Veridian Pro Cancellation and Account Closure

This document explains how to cancel Veridian Pro service and close an account with Veridian Software. It is intended to provide clear procedural guidance for account owners and administrators. It does not describe pricing or any financial outcomes; it focuses only on the steps required to end access.

Cancellations must be submitted via the account portal or by contacting Veridian Software support. The account portal provides a structured workflow to submit a cancellation request and confirm the request details. Customers who prefer direct assistance may contact support and request cancellation by email or through the official support channel listed in their account.

All cancellation requests must include the customer account ID. The account ID is required to verify the request and to ensure that the correct workspace is identified. The ID can be found in the portal under account settings, and it is also visible on the customer profile screen. Requests that do not include the account ID will require follow-up before they can be processed.

When a cancellation request is submitted, the account owner will receive a confirmation notice. This notice confirms that the request was received and provides a reference number for tracking. The confirmation does not represent a change in account status; it is simply a record that the request has been logged by Veridian Software.

Account closure removes access to Veridian Pro and deactivates associated user accounts. Before closure, administrators should export any required data and notify team members of the planned change. Veridian Software recommends reviewing connected integrations and access tokens to ensure that any external workflows are updated accordingly.

If a customer has multiple workspaces or divisions under a single organization, each workspace must be canceled separately. The cancellation request should identify the specific workspace to avoid unintended changes. Support can assist with multi-workspace requests if the account owner provides the relevant account IDs.

Cancellation requests are handled by the support team or by the automated portal workflow depending on the submission method. Verification steps may be required to confirm ownership and authority. These steps protect the account from unauthorized changes and ensure that only authorized users can close an account.

This document describes the operational process for cancellation and account closure. It is designed to be predictable and consistent for all customers. For the most current instructions, customers should use the account portal or contact support directly with their account ID and request details.

# Veridian Pro Promotional Discounts and Limitations

This document describes the promotional pricing rules for Veridian Pro and how discounts are applied. It explains the limitations associated with promotional offers and clarifies how promotional pricing interacts with standard terms. The intent is to ensure customers understand the scope and boundaries of temporary pricing incentives.

Promotional offers may include percentage discounts, fixed-amount reductions, or bundled incentives tied to a specific campaign. Each promotion has a defined eligibility window and may be limited to new customers or specific regions. Promotional offers are not guaranteed and may be withdrawn or modified at the discretion of Veridian Software.

Purchases made under promotional pricing are non-refundable. This limitation applies to orders that used a promotional code, discounted rate, or bundled incentive. The promotional terms are provided at the time of purchase and are included in the order confirmation. Customers should review those terms before completing a promotional purchase.

Promotions apply only to the specific product and plan listed in the offer. They do not automatically extend to add-on services, third-party products, or future plan changes. If a customer upgrades or changes plans, the promotional rate may not transfer to the new plan unless the offer explicitly states otherwise.

Promotional discounts do not alter the standard feature set of Veridian Pro. Customers on a promotional rate receive the same product capabilities as customers on standard pricing. However, promotional rates may have specific usage limits or campaign-specific constraints, which are detailed in the offer.

Promotional pricing typically applies for a defined term and then reverts to the standard rate unless the offer explicitly states otherwise. Customers will see the promotional term and the standard rate in the order confirmation. Veridian Software recommends reviewing these details to avoid surprises when the promotional period ends.

Certain promotions may be limited to specific industries, regions, or customer segments. Eligibility details are stated in the offer and may require verification during checkout. If a promotion is applied outside the stated eligibility, Veridian Software may adjust the order to the standard rate and notify the customer of the correction.

Veridian Software may limit promotions to one per customer account. Multiple promotions cannot be combined unless the offer explicitly permits stacking. If more than one promotion is applied in error, the company may adjust the pricing to align with the intended campaign terms.

This document covers promotional pricing limitations only. It does not describe plan terms, account procedures, or payment processing timelines. For full details on plan structure and standard pricing, customers should consult the product catalog or the customer portal.

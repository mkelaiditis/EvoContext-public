# Veridian Pro Policy Amendments and Effective Dates

This document explains how Veridian Software updates policies that govern Veridian Pro. It describes how policy amendments are communicated and how effective dates are applied. The goal is to provide transparency so customers understand when changes take effect and how they are announced.

Veridian Software may update policies from time to time to reflect product changes, regulatory requirements, or operational improvements. When a policy is updated, Veridian Software provides notice through the customer portal, email notifications, or published updates in the product documentation. The notice includes the effective date of the change and a summary of the revision.

Policy changes apply prospectively, not retroactively. This means that updates affect future activity or future billing periods after the effective date. The terms in place before the effective date continue to govern actions that occurred earlier. This approach ensures that customers are not subject to unexpected changes for past activity.

Customers are encouraged to review policy updates when they are announced. The policy revision history is maintained in the customer portal so that organizations can track changes over time. Veridian Software also provides a summary of significant amendments to help administrators understand the impact on their organization.

Veridian Software keeps prior versions of policies available for reference to support audits and internal compliance reviews. The archived versions include the effective dates and a brief change summary. This record helps customers understand which terms applied to historical activity and supports internal governance processes.

Notice of policy changes typically includes a summary of key sections affected and the date when the updated terms take effect. Customers should ensure that their account contact information is current so that notices are delivered to the appropriate recipients. If a notice cannot be delivered, the update is still published in the portal with the effective date for reference.

If a customer has questions about a policy change, they can contact support for clarification. Support can provide guidance on where to find the updated policy and how to interpret the effective date. For complex organizational needs, account managers may assist with clarifying how updates align with specific contracts or usage patterns.

This document focuses on how policies are amended and how effective dates are applied. It does not introduce new eligibility conditions or account procedures. Customers should consult the relevant policy document for the full terms that apply to their use of Veridian Pro.
